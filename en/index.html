About me

Sofie Panfilova

I'm a clinical dietitian, an expert in preventive medicine and longevity, a Global Wellness ambassador and HealthTech & MedTech consultant. For over a decade I've been helping people improve their health by preventing diseases before they appear.

My journey began with clinical dietetics: I graduated from the First Faculty of Medicine at Charles University in Prague, then earned an MBA in healthcare management. For many years I worked with patients, studied the foundations of longevity, and created nutritional and recovery programs at the premium rehabilitation clinic HIVE and the private medical centers American Medical Centers, while also gaining experience in supplements at Health Labs Care.

Over time I outgrew purely clinical practice. I increasingly saw that conventional medicine reacts to consequences rather than preventing the causes of disease. This led me into digital health — where medicine meets technology. In the Czech Republic I became one of the first to implement personalized health solutions based on telemedicine SaaS platforms.

A key milestone was working with the Israeli company Biognostica, which developed a cross-analysis blood technology that enables deeper interpretation of lab results. This experience shaped my approach to health as a system that can be managed and improved.

This gave rise to my exclusive project 'Health as a Project' — a methodology where health becomes a structured system with clear metrics, an improvement strategy, and predictable results. Just like managing a successful company, only the subject is the human body.

Today I create personalized longevity programs for private clients worldwide and consult on HealthTech and MedTech projects. My global mission is to help people live longer, more actively, and better by preventing disease before it starts.

Familiar symptoms?

The root cause may be deeper than you think

Many symptoms are the body's signals about hidden disruptions that standard tests don't always reveal.

Chronic fatigue

Often linked to micronutrient deficiencies, thyroid dysfunction, or mitochondrial issues.

Weight issues

Weight gain or sudden loss may indicate insulin resistance, hormonal shifts, or chronic inflammation.

Hormonal imbalance

Mood swings, irritability, decreased libido — may result from imbalances easily corrected with proper diagnostics.

Digestive issues

Bloating, swelling, discomfort — often the result of food intolerances, gut dysbiosis, or systemic inflammation.

Pregnancy & breastfeeding anxiety

What to eat, what to avoid, how to replenish deficiencies — without a dietitian's support, anxiety only grows.

Tests 'normal', but you feel off

Standard tests don't always show the full picture. In-depth biomarker interpretation reveals hidden problems.

Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How does your practice work?

I work with what often gets overlooked: fatigue, weight gain, chronic stress, declining health markers. These seem 'not urgent' — but over time develop into serious problems requiring much more effort and expense to resolve. My approach is science-based prevention: we identify risks before diagnoses appear and gently correct the body's condition. I thoroughly analyze your lab results and build a personalized strategy — nutrition, supplements, daily support. No universal templates — only what works for you specifically.

I've already invested a lot in my health and regularly get tested. Would your program still be useful?

Most likely, yes. Tests are data, but the real value lies in their proper interpretation and actionable follow-through. I'll help turn your accumulated information into a working strategy: adjusting nutrition, lifestyle, and habits so you're not just 'getting tested' but genuinely improving your quality of life.

How is your approach different from a regular nutritionist at a clinic?

Three key differences. First, depth: each case is analyzed individually and holistically — what worked for one client isn't copied to another. Second, continuous connection: I'm always available, not just during appointments. This helps maintain motivation, quickly resolve questions, and reinforce new habits. Third, attention to detail — I treat every client as if they're my only one.

Are tests included in the program price?

No. All tests are selected individually, and you take them at a lab near you. I'll help you choose the right laboratory.

What guarantees do you offer?

I work for results. If improvements don't come despite following all recommendations — I'll refund your money. My approach is always comprehensive and personal: we look at the situation globally, not locally. Everything works online: no travel needed, I'm always available. And it's a smart investment: by investing in your health now, you save on treating chronic conditions in the future.

I don't have much free time. Can I still do the program?

Yes. Everything is online, and I adjust to your schedule. Just 10–15 minutes a day for diet tracking and adjustments is enough. I also work on weekends, when we can dedicate more time to your questions.

Can you help with chronic conditions?

Yes. I'll design a diet plan considering your diagnoses and blood work to reduce symptoms and improve well-being. I'll also provide lifestyle recommendations to help your body cope with the load more effectively.

What if I have no health complaints — why would I need this?

Exactly so that complaints don't appear. Modern pace of life, stress, and unbalanced diet affect everyone. Even if everything is fine now, we'll build a strategy to strengthen your current state and extend active longevity — from nutrition and environment analysis to selecting modern health monitoring gadgets. I can also offer programs for your family and parents.

Do you work with pregnant and breastfeeding women?

Yes, this is one of my key specializations. I help with nutrition by trimester, replenishing deficiencies, selecting safe supplements, and creating breastfeeding nutrition plans.

Can you help with nutrition while on Ozempic / Wegovy / Mounjaro?

Yes. GLP-1 medications significantly reduce appetite, and many people lose not just fat but also muscle mass. I'll help build a nutrition plan that preserves muscle, ensures adequate nutrients despite reduced appetite, and prepares you for comfortable medication discontinuation without rebound weight gain.

Where should I start if I'm not sure what I need?

Start with the express diagnostics for €97 — a 30-minute review of your situation or a written analysis of your results. You'll get concrete answers and understand whether you need to go further. It's the simplest and fastest way to find out.
