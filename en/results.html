М.К.

Marina, 42 · Limassol

Chronic fatigue

«For the first time in two years, I wake up without fatigue»

Chief Complaint

Chronic fatigue, +7 kg gained in 18 months despite stable diet. Three previous dietitians with no results. Standard lab tests 'normal.'

Findings

Ferritin 18 ng/mL with 'normal' hemoglobin. Early-stage insulin resistance. Vitamin D deficiency — 14 ng/mL.

Result

In 8 weeks: −4.2 kg, ferritin 62 ng/mL, vitamin D — 48 ng/mL. Energy from 3/10 to 8/10. Afternoon crashes at 3pm ended.

8 weeks · Nutritional Support
И.М.

Irina, 52 · Prague

Menopause & HRT

«My doctor prescribed HRT and said eat healthy food. Sofia explained exactly what.»

Chief Complaint

Menopause, HRT for 6 months. Fat redistributed to abdomen, 8-10 hot flashes daily, muscle loss, insomnia. No specialist explained how to eat with HRT.

Findings

Critically low protein intake (0.6 g/kg). Magnesium and B6 deficiency. Chronic sleep deprivation elevated cortisol.

Result

In 12 weeks: hot flashes reduced to 1-2 daily. Sleep normalized. −3.8 kg fat loss with preserved muscle. Finally had a protocol that worked with HRT, not against it.

12 weeks · Nutritional Support
А.Р.

Pregnancy

«I was afraid of harming my baby with every meal. Sofia removed the anxiety and gave me a plan.»

Chief Complaint

First pregnancy abroad. Morning sickness until week 16, fear of gestational diabetes, conflicting internet advice. Local doctor advised to relax and not worry.

Iron deficiency at baseline (ferritin 15 ng/mL). Suboptimal vitamin D. Diet didn't meet second trimester requirements.

Individualized trimester-by-trimester plan. Iron normalized without IV supplementation. Weight gain within normal range. No gestational diabetes. Calm, informed pregnancy.

С.Д.

Svetlana, 45 · Prague

GLP-1 therapy

«I lost 14 kg on Ozempic, but I was losing muscle. Sofia stopped it.»

Chief Complaint

On GLP-1 (Ozempic) for 5 months. Lost 14 kg but also losing muscle. Appetite so suppressed that eating only 700 kcal daily. Weakness, loose skin, fear of regain.

Findings

Protein intake — 28 g/day (need 80+ g). Critical B12 and zinc deficiency. Lean mass loss — 4.1 kg in 5 months.

Result

Nutrition protocol adapted to suppressed appetite: small-volume meals with high nutrient density. In 8 weeks: protein consistently 75+ g/day, muscle loss stopped, B12 and zinc normalized.

8 weeks · Nutritional Support
