Express Nutrition Diagnostics

30-minute video call or written analysis of your test results with personalized recommendations

€97
30 minutes

Express Nutrition Diagnostics is a fast and precise way to understand your situation without committing to a long program. If you feel that something is off with your nutrition or well-being but do not know where to start, this service is designed for you.

You can choose one of two formats. The first is a 30-minute one-on-one video call. During this time, we will review your current situation: nutrition, symptoms, and lifestyle. You will receive concrete steps that you can start implementing right away, along with a list of tests that are truly necessary for you specifically — not a generic panel covering everything at random.

The second format is a written analysis. You fill out a detailed questionnaire, attach your test results, and describe your symptoms and goals. I analyze everything comprehensively and send you a written report with interpretation of results, nutrition recommendations, and a list of additional tests if needed.

This format is ideal for women who are tired of feeling tired, experience unclear symptoms, gain weight without obvious reasons, or simply want a professional assessment of their condition. It is also an excellent first step for those considering longer support programs but wanting to understand what is actually going on first.

No generic advice. Everything you receive is specific, based on your individual situation. Over 10 years of clinical practice, I have learned to identify connections between symptoms, test results, and lifestyle that are typically overlooked in a standard approach.

If after the express diagnostics you decide to continue working together, the cost of this service will be credited toward an extended consultation or support program.

Frequently Asked Questions

How should I prepare for the express diagnostics?

Fill out the questionnaire and attach recent test results (if available). For the video call — prepare your questions. For the written format — describe your symptoms and goals in detail.

How is express diagnostics different from a full consultation?

Express diagnostics is a quick start: in 30 minutes you get direction and first steps. A full consultation (1.5 hours) includes deep anamnesis, a nutrition strategy, and a follow-up session.

Will I receive a meal plan?

You will receive personalized recommendations and a list of necessary tests. A detailed meal plan is included in the Nutrition Consultation or Nutritional Support services.
